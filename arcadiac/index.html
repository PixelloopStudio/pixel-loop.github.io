Arcadiac by Pixelloop

Arcadiac is an arcade style fun game by Pixelloop.


Your goal is to bring as many objects in the scene as possible. When the screen is full of objects you lose the game. Watch out! You should shoot the object before the arrow gets red! Objects with the same shape and same color will delete each other, so you get some free space in the game!

Buy Powerups in the Shop


Use the money you’ve earned from playing to buy useful items in the shop! These items will bring you a big advantage to set your new highscore. Currently you can choose between 3 items (more will be added). Shrink, Undo and Explosion. Shrink will make your object smaller. Undo will make you undo your last shot. Explosion will make your object a bomb that destroys nearby objects with it.

Use Powerups



Tap on the object in the middle of the screen to show your inventory. Select your item of choice. Press return and get back to business! Are you ready for a new highscore?

Advertisment Friendly



This App is, like all other Pixelloop apps, free from annoying advertisements. You can choose by 100% if you want to see that advertisement right now or not. Sure, you’ll get a small reward for watching it, but that's just how the math works.
