Arcadiac by Pixelloop

Arcadiac is an arcade style fun game by Pixelloop.

Your goal is to bring as many objects on the screen as possible. When the screen is full of objects you lose the game. Objects with the same shape and same color will delete each other, so you get more free space in the game!

Test and train your survival skills
Arcadiac is a very fast and casual game. You have to decide quickly where you want to shoot your next object.
The arrow, which is moving around your object,
fades from a white to a red color.
Once the arrow is completely red,
the object gets shoot automatically wherever the arrow is pointing at.

Become the best in Arcadiac
Compete against players all over the world!
We included a global leaderboard where you can
compete against all your friends and show your skills all over the world.
Do you have what it takes to become number one?

Progress and power-ups
While playing Arcadiac you'll earn ingame-money.
You can use that money to buy power-ups in the shop.
You can choose between three power-ups.
The shrink-power-up: this allows you to make your current object smaller.
The undo-power-up: this allows you to undo your last shot.
The explosion-power-up: this allows you to transform your current object into a bomb which will destroy surrounding objects when colliding.

Advertisement friendly
This App is, like all other Pixelloop apps, free from annoying advertisements.
You can choose by 100% if you want to see that advertisement right now or not.
